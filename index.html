Artificial Sweetener Final Report

Neotame is a type of Artificial Sweetener used in baked goods, soft drinks, yogurt, chewing gum, candies, frozen desserts, powdered drink mixes, and fruit spreads. It is also used in confectionery and as a flavor enhancer. It sweetens 7000–13,000x more than any other sweetener, is non-cancerigenous, non-cariogenic as it gives 0 kcal, has a standard p.H level of 4.5 which means it is close to being a neutral substance and doesn’t pose a risk to people with an autosomal, recessive metabolic disorder called phenylketonuria. Thus, it can be a good, safe source of attraction for youth markets.

A study in the U.K. suggests this artificial sweetener damages healthy cells in the human intestinal tract, leading to serious health problems such as irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), which is classified as a genetic disease caused by a single inherited gene mutation whose symptoms can be contributed by consuming the substance. Neotame can alter healthy cells in human intestinal walls, which can impact the integrity of the gut wall. The sweetener directly damages epithelial cells in the intestinal wall, and adversely affects bacteria in the gut. This was done observing two bacteria’s reactions to the substance, E. Coli and E. Faecalis.

Financial benefits include a lower cost for greater sweetness, meaning a lower amount of Neotame needed to satisfy each soft drink. The cost of Neotame for 1kg ranges from $10 to $60, averaging about $27, whereas Stevia can range the same price ($10 to $60), however Stevia has lower sweetness and a greater price, meaning Neotame is more cost-effective. Risks include loss of market, since prolonged sweetness in taste after consumption may not be many clients’ favourite, which means adding Neotame in soft drinks relies on the market being sold to.

I recommend Neotame being the artificial sweetener for this company. Its benefits outweigh the risks. Even though gut health risks (IBS-causing) and certain customers’ feedback could affect some both health and financial logistics, because of its efficiency in greater sweetness for a smaller amount given it is a safe substance for daily consumption, it is worth adding it to the drink’s flavour and ingredients. If the right market is selected for this drink, the new taste will be a boost for this company.
