Neotame is a type of Artificial Sweetener used in baked goods, soft drinks, yogurt, chewing gum, candies, frozen desserts, powdered drink mixes, and fruit spreads. It is also used in confectionery and as a flavor enhancer. It sweetens 7000–13,000x more than any other sweetener, is non-cancerigenous, non-cariogenic as it gives 0 kcal, has a standard p.H level of 4.5 which means it is close to being a neutral substance and doesn’t pose a risk to people with an autosomal, recessive metabolic disorder called phenylketonuria. Thus, it can be a good, safe source of attraction for youth markets.
